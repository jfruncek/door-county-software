These jars must be added to the local repo because they are not available in a public Maven repo.
(See also identical .cmd and .sh files)

Missing jars: Maven issues

mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=javax.jms -DartifactId=jms -Dversion=1.1 -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=jms.jar
mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=com.sun.jdmk -DartifactId=jmxtools -Dversion=1.2.1 -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=jmxtools.jar
mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=com.sun.jmx -DartifactId=jmxri -Dversion=1.2.1 -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=jmxri.jar
mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=xmlbeans -DartifactId=xmlbeans -Dversion=2.4.0 -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=xmlbeans-2.4.0.jar

Missing jars: Open Reports issues

mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=net.socialchange.doctype -DartifactId=doctype-changer -Dversion=1.1 -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=DoctypeChanger.jar
mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=net.sf.docbook -DartifactId=docbook -Dversion=1.74.0 -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=docbook.jar

mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=com.googlcode.strut2gwtplugin -DartifactId=struts2gwtplugin -Dversion=1.02 -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=struts2gwtplugin-1.02.jar
mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=javassist -DartifactId=javassist -Dversion=3.12.1.GA -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=javassist-3.12.1.GA.jar

Missing jars: Jasper Reports (obtain from their site)
mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=jasperreports -DartifactId=jasperreports -Dversion=4.0.1 -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=jasperreports-4.0.1.jar

Missing jars: Database drivers (you must own or obtain these)
mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=com.oracle -DartifactId=ojdbc6 -Dversion=11.2.0.1 -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=ojdbc6.jar
mvn install:install-file -DgroupId=com.microsoft -DartifactId=sqljdbc4 -Dversion=1.0 -Dpackaging=jar -Dfile=sqljdbc4.jar
